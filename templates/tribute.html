Toby Fox

Toby Fox, depicted in the above image, is a Video Game Developer and Composer. He is famous for the popular Undertale game, which he released in 2015.

Current timeline for Toby Fox:

October 11, 1991

Toby Fox was born

2009

Toby studied Environmental Science at Northeastern University in Boston, Massachusetts, and had started composing a variety of music for Andrew Hussie's 2009 webcomic Homestuck during his senior year of high school. Though he did not initially respond when Hussie started a "Music Contribution Team" in April 2009 and put up a news post asking musicians to participate, Hussie took note of his work when Fox started uploading piano covers of the webcomic's music on the MS Paint Adventures forums

2012

Deltarune Development started, although it was abandoned before Fox completed one room. According to a 2019 interview held to promote the game's Nintendo Switch release, the idea for Deltarune came to Fox in a dream he had in 2011 while he was at college. In the dream, he saw the ending to a video game, and was determined to create it

2013

Undertale was developed by Toby Fox across 32 months. Development was financed through a crowdfunding campaign on the website Kickstarter. The campaign was launched on June 25, 2013 with a goal of $5,000

2014

Undertale was initially meant to be two hours in length and was set to be released in mid-2014, but development was delayed

2015

Undertale was released for Microsoft Windows and OS X in September 2015. Undertale is a game remembered for its great story telling, unique writing, different combat system from other known games. The game has amazing music which brings the game to life. There isn't a game like Undertale, with its originality, and you can see this with the character design inside of the game. The game also has multiple endings for the loyal fans that want to come back for another run. Undertale sold over one million copies after its release, and was nominated for multiple accolades and awards, and was even awarded Game of the Year from several publications

2016

Undertale was released for Linux in July 2016

2017

Undertale was released for PlayStation 4 and PlayStation Vita in August 2017

2018

Undertale was released for Nintendo Switch in September 2018. Fox released the first chapter of Deltarune, a game "intended for people who have completed Undertale", on October 31, 2018 for Windows and macOS for free. Deltarune was developed by Fox in GameMaker Studio 2. The game introduces a new battle system influenced by the Final Fantasy franchise, contrasting with the original game's combat system (which was inspired by that of the Mother series). Some of the music in the game remixes much of the soundtrack of Undertale, but most pieces are new
